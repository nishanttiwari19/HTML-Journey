Discover Bhopal

Bhopal is the capital city of Madhya Pradesh, India. It is known for its beautiful lakes, historical monuments, and vibrant culture.

Top Attractions in Bhopal

  • Upper Lake (Bhojtal): As the oldest man-made lake in India, this spot is perfect for boating, scenic views, and picnics, often considered the heart of the city.
  • Van Vihar National Park: Located adjacent to the Upper Lake, this zoo-cum-national park provides a natural habitat for animals like lions and tigers, allowing visitors to walk, cycle, or drive through
  • Taj-ul-Masajid: One of the largest mosques in Asia, this pink-sandstone structure is a stunning example of Islamic architecture, featuring a large courtyard and impressive minarets.
  • Madhya Pradesh Tribal Museum: A unique, award-winning museum that showcases the vibrant art, culture, and lifestyles of the state's tribal communities in an immersive setting
